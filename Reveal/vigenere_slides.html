Encryption and Decryption involve the Vigenère Square, which consists of combination of multiple shifts of Caesar Cipher:

Encryption - Rules

  1. Every letter in the plaintext corresponds with the letter in keyword in regards to its position.
  2. Encryption is done, one letter of the plaintext and one letter of keyword, at one time.
  3. The letter in keyword correspond to the same letter in Vigenere Square's ROW
  4. The letter in plaintext correspond to the same letter in Vignere Square's COLUMN
  5. The intersected letter will be the ciphertext for that letter in plaintext.
